# Methodology

The v2 methodology separates three dimensions that were previously blended:

- Importance now: current impact, magnitude, urgency, actor weight, and decision
  relevance.
- Future potential: novelty, acceleration, diffusion, actor diversity, real
  commitment, cross-domain portability, and option value.
- Evidence confidence: source quality, primary-source support, claim coverage,
  data-method quality, recency, independence, and counterevidence handling.

Collection uses two lanes:

- Top-down lane: official, regulator, company, exchange, international
  organization, wire, and national sources.
- Bottom-up lane: local media, small company releases, research prototypes,
  developer tools, public community channels, procurement, hiring, patents, and
  other weak-signal sources.

Daily outputs use slot caps for readability. Slot caps are not completeness
proof. A report is complete only when the run budget, source health, coverage
cells, evidence trace, rejection counters, and report contract are validated.
